Aviary Canvas is a fully offline utility app for planning bird enclosures. No account, no login, and no internet connection are required at any point — the app works entirely on-device.
How to test core functionality:
1. Open the app. A splash screen appears for 5 seconds (or tap to dismiss).
2. Tap Canvas in the bottom navigation to open the enclosure planner. Tap the grid to draw enclosure zones — assign each zone a type (feeding, nesting, roosting, etc.) and a bird count. The calculator validates spacing requirements in real time.
3. Tap Calculator to use the standalone perch calculator — enter species and bird count to get recommended perch height and rail spacing.
4. Tap Profile to view XP, rank, streaks, and progress.
5. Tap Achievements to see the 17 achievements and 7 trophies.
No special setup required. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, sign-in flows, or network requests. All data is stored locally on the device.
