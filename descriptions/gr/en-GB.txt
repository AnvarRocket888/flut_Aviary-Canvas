You have a patch of ground and a flock of birds that deserve better than a cramped wire pen. Aviary Canvas starts you with a blank grid and the tools to turn that patch into a properly planned home.
Draw the outline of your enclosure. Fill zones for feeding, drinking, nesting, roosting, and outdoor runs. Add a storage corner. Label each zone and set the number of birds it will serve. In the same moment you draw, the app checks the maths. Every bird gets enough floor space. Every group has access to a feeder and a water point. Predators and prey stay on separate sides.
The perch calculator handles the detail that most keepers overlook. Type in your species and count. The app returns the correct perch height and the right spacing between rails, so the birds can sleep in peace instead of fighting for position.
When the design is ready, save it. Come back and edit it later. Export a written summary to hand to a builder, or keep it as a reference for the real construction.
The more you plan, the more the app rewards you. Experience points accumulate with every action. Ranks rise from Hatchling to Master Aviarist. Streaks count the days you return. Seventeen achievements mark the milestones, and seven trophies wait at the peaks.
Aviary Canvas is fully offline. No account is required. No subscription. No internet connection. Just you, your birds, and a plan that works.
